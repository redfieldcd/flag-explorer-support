Flag Explorer - Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

You can use Flag Explorer free for 10 minutes. After that, a one-time purchase of $3.99 unlocks unlimited access forever.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you've already purchased Flag Explorer, tap "Restore Purchase" on the paywall screen. Your purchase is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.

How many countries are included?

Flag Explorer includes flags from all 197 countries, organized by region: Africa, Asia, Europe, North America, South America, and Oceania.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your progress is stored locally on your device. We do not collect any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
