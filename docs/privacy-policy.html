Flag Explorer - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Overview

Flag Explorer ("the App") is an educational app for learning flags from countries around the world. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

Flag Explorer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data, including your learning progress and usage time, is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple's StoreKit framework to process in-app purchases. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your payment information.

Analytics and Tracking

Flag Explorer does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage, location, or any other personal information.

Children's Privacy

Flag Explorer is designed to be safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users.

Data Storage

All data (learning progress, quiz scores, usage time) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data is not accessible to us and is removed when you delete the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
